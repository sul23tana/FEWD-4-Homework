Misk Foundation, established by HRH Prince Mohammed bin Salman, is a non-profit foundation devoted to cultivate and encourage learning and leadership in youth for a better future in Saudi Arabia. Misk Foundation invests in empowering youth in four main pillars; education, media, culture & technology. These pillars of knowledge will support and advance our country’s future.
