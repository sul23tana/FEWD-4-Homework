Creating Opportunities to Develop Society and Unleashing Individuals’ Power

We enable the society to learn, develop and progress in business, literature and culture, and science and technology through establishing incubators and encouraging esteemed institutions to provide an attractive and stimulating environment.

Misk Foundation, established by HRH Prince Mohammed bin Salman, is a non-profit foundation devoted to cultivate and encourage learning and leadership in youth for a better future in Saudi Arabia. To this end, Misk focuses on the country’s youth and provides various means to foster and empower talents, creative potential, and innovation and create a healthy environment for them to grow and develop. Misk also seeks to pave the way towards opportunities in the fields of art and science. Misk Foundation invests in empowering youth in four main pillars; education, media, culture & technology. These pillars of knowledge will support and advance our country’s future. Enabling Saudi youth to learn is a means through which advancements across the business, technological, literary, cultural, and social aspects of our nation are made.
