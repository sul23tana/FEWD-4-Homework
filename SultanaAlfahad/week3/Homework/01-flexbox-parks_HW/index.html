About the National Park System

Learn More

"National parks are the best idea we ever had. Absolutely American, absolutely democratic, they reflect us at our best rather than our worst."

So wrote author Wallace Stegner in 1983. We invite you to discover why. From the rich heritage of peoples and places, to the incredible natural resources in each and every park, to the incredible natural beauty of the American landscape, the 59 national parks across the United States showcase the best of the country.
